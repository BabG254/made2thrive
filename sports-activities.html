Sports Activities Program

Building Healthy Bodies, Strong Minds, and Team Spirit

Support This Program

More Than Just Games

Our Sports Activities Program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and character development through organized sports and recreational activities. We believe that sports teach valuable life lessons including discipline, perseverance, leadership, and respect for others. Our program provides opportunities for youth to develop athletic skills while building confidence and forming lasting friendships in a supportive environment.

Sports & Activities

Football (Soccer)

The most popular sport, promoting teamwork, coordination, and strategic thinking.

Basketball

Fast-paced game that builds agility, hand-eye coordination, and quick decision-making.

Volleyball

Team sport that emphasizes communication, timing, and collaborative strategy.

Track & Field

Individual and team events that build endurance, speed, and personal achievement.

Table Tennis

Precision sport that develops reflexes, concentration, and mental agility.

Fitness Training

General fitness activities that build strength, flexibility, and overall health.

Why Sports Matter

Physical Benefits

Cardiovascular Health

Regular physical activity strengthens the heart and improves overall fitness levels.

Strength & Coordination

Sports activities develop muscle strength, balance, and motor coordination skills.

Healthy Weight Management

Active participation helps maintain healthy body weight and prevents obesity.

Better Sleep & Energy

Physical activity improves sleep quality and increases overall energy levels.

Life Skills Development

Teamwork & Cooperation

Learning to work together toward common goals and support teammates.

Leadership Skills

Opportunities to captain teams and guide others in achieving success.

Discipline & Time Management

Regular training schedules teach punctuality and commitment to goals.

Respect & Fair Play

Understanding rules, respecting opponents, and playing with integrity.

Training Programs

Beginner Level

Introduction to basic skills, rules, and fundamentals of various sports.

Age Group: 8-12 years

Focus: Fun & Basic Skills

Duration: 2 hours/week

Intermediate Level

Skill development, strategy learning, and competitive play introduction.

Age Group: 13-16 years

Focus: Skill Development

Duration: 3 hours/week

Advanced Level

Competitive training, leadership development, and tournament preparation.

Age Group: 17+ years

Focus: Competition & Leadership

Duration: 4 hours/week

Competitions & Events

Annual Sports Festival

Our biggest sports event featuring competitions in all sports, bringing together youth from across the region.

When: Every December
Participants: 200+ athletes
Events: 15+ different sports
Prizes: Medals & scholarships

Inter-School Leagues

Regular competitions between schools to maintain engagement and provide ongoing challenges for athletes.

Frequency: Monthly tournaments
Schools: 15 participating schools
Sports: Football, Basketball, Volleyball
Recognition: Trophies & certificates
