Youth Camps Program

Building Character Through Adventure and Learning

Support This Program

Fun, Learning, and Character Development

Our Youth Camps provide a perfect blend of fun, learning, and character development. During school holidays, we organize camps that engage young people in meaningful activities while building essential life skills. These camps create safe spaces where youth can explore their interests, develop leadership qualities, and form lasting friendships while learning valuable lessons about teamwork, responsibility, and community service.

Camp Activities

Sports & Fitness

Football, basketball, athletics, and team sports that promote physical fitness and teamwork.

Arts & Crafts

Creative workshops including painting, drawing, music, and traditional crafts.

Leadership Training

Activities designed to develop leadership skills, confidence, and decision-making abilities.

Environmental Projects

Tree planting, cleanup campaigns, and conservation activities that teach environmental responsibility.

Life Skills Workshops

Sessions on communication, problem-solving, financial literacy, and career planning.

Adventure Activities

Hiking, camping, outdoor challenges that build resilience and confidence.

Skills We Develop

Personal Development

Self-Confidence

Building belief in one's abilities through successful completion of challenges and activities.

Resilience

Learning to overcome obstacles and bounce back from setbacks with determination.

Independence

Developing the ability to make decisions and take responsibility for actions.

Goal Setting

Learning to set achievable goals and create plans to accomplish them.

Social Skills

Communication

Developing effective verbal and non-verbal communication skills in various settings.

Teamwork

Learning to collaborate effectively with others toward common goals.

Leadership

Developing the ability to guide, motivate, and inspire others.

Conflict Resolution

Learning peaceful ways to resolve disagreements and misunderstandings.

Camp Schedule

Duration: 7 days

Age Group: 13-19 years

Capacity: 60 participants
