About Us

Made to Thrive is rooted in Kenya and shaped by experience in the West. We provide education, character formation (spiritual and moral), and personal development that helps the next generation think critically, solve real problems, grow in resilient character, and lead change in their communities and beyond.

Education

School visits, university tours, and learning opportunities

Character

Spiritual and moral formation through mentorship

Community

Sports, camps, and service projects that bring people together
