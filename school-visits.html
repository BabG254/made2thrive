Making Education Accessible and Exciting

Our School Visits Program is the cornerstone of our mission to empower youth through education. We regularly visit schools to provide direct support, resources, and inspiration to students and educators. Through these visits, we have managed to bridge educational gaps and created opportunities for young people to discover their unique potential.

What We Provide

Educational Resources

A community library with diverse resources to enhance everyone's learning experience.

Teacher Training

Progressive professional training equips teachers with the tools needed to thrive in today's education landscape, which presents unique challenges to the teachers and their students.

Student Mentorship

One-on-one coaching and direction to help students make informed sustainable decisions, especially towards their career choices. Our program has resulted in reduced college dropout by 25%. It has also increased the uptake for technical colleges and diploma programs by 40%. This translates into an accelerated path to career sucess and decongestion of the job market.

Motivational Talks

There's immense power in authentic storytelling. The founder of Made 2 Thrive, Ms. Rosemary Nzembi, is a Certified professional Life Coach, and has mastered the art of storytelling. Through group storytelling and Inspirational talks, our students find encouragement to overcome their social, mental, and societal challenges. Storytelling fosters a warm learning atmosphere, therefore making it possible to share some hard yet crucial information with our students.

Program Impact

Success Stories

Improved Academic Performance

The schools we've collaborated with have shown an average of 35% improvement in student performance and overall confidence.

Increased Attendance

Student participation rate stands at about 25%, up from zero, which was the case before our program was introduced.

Teacher Motivation

Improved student performance, conducive learning environment and staff recognition can increase their motivation, therefore leading to increased productivity.
