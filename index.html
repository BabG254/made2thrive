Our Mission

Made to Thrive is a global voice for women and young people. Rooted in Kenya and shaped by experience in the West, we provide education, character (spiritual and moral) formation, and personal development that helps the next generation think critically, solve real problems, grow in resilient character, and lead change in their communities and beyond.
